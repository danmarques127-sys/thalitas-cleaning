Move-In / Move-Out & Post-Construction Cleaning in Massachusetts

Moving, selling or finishing a renovation? Thalita’s Cleaning provides detailed cleaning for empty homes, rentals and new construction — including inside cabinets, appliances, doors, trim, high ledges and construction dust. Serving Marlborough, Worcester, Framingham and Greater Boston.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Whether you are a tenant, landlord, realtor or property manager, our move-in/move-out cleaning was designed to pass walkthroughs and leave the property 100% ready for the next person.

We clean inside cabinets and drawers, appliances (fridge, oven, microwave), doors, baseboards, light switches, windowsills and hard-to-reach areas — so nothing is left behind from the previous occupant.

Get Move-Out Pricing

Post-Construction / Renovation Cleaning

Remodeling projects leave dust everywhere: on trim, doors, window tracks, vents, light fixtures and even on top of cabinets. Our post-construction cleaning removes fine dust, stickers, paint splatters and job-site residue to make the space ready for delivery.

Perfect for homeowners, contractors and small builders who need a final clean before inspection or move-in. We can also come back for a polish visit after the dust settles.
